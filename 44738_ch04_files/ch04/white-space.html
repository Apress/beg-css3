Controlling Whitespace

Twinkle, twinkle, little bat! How I wonder what you're at! Up above the world you fly, Like a tea-tray in the sky.

Text normally wraps onto the next line when it reaches the edge of its container.

Setting white-space to nowrap keeps this all on the same line

pre-line: Close up sequences of whitespace as in normal text, but honor new lines.

pre-wrap: Preserve sequences of whitespace and new lines, but automatically wrap text when the border is reached.
